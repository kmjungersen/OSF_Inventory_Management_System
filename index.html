Welcome to The OSF's Laboratory Inventory Management System (OSF-LIMS)

The OSF is already a fantastic tool for researchers, and it's about to get even better. With an inventory that's constantly growing and changing, you need a way to keep track of everything that comes in and out of stock. So now we're introducing an RFID based system, for fast, simple, and accurate material tracking.

Please check back soon for more updates on the development of this fantastic product!
